# Entity

## Notebook

One physical sketch notebook.

Fixed 60 physical pages at creation
Pages still inside the notebook only
Torn pages disappear from here

## Page

A page still bound in a notebook.

Contains multiple text blocks
Has character limit
Can be torn out exactly once

## LooseLeaf

A page after tearing out.

Editable after recall
Lives outside any notebook
May be clipped into a group

## ClipGroup

A paperclip group of loose leaves.

Contains only loose leaves
Represents grouping, not copying
Recall returns the group and its pages

# Transitions

**Notebook** owns many **Page**

tearOut(page) turns one **Page** into one **LooseLeaf**

clip(looseLeaves) creates one **ClipGroup**
